Meeting: Sprint Planning - AI Workflow Automation
Date: 2026-04-24

Discussion:
The team discussed building an AI workflow automation prototype to support Scrum Masters and delivery teams. The main goal is to reduce manual sprint reporting and improve delivery visibility.

Current Challenges:
- Scrum Masters manually collect task data from Jira.
- Weekly sprint reports take too much time to prepare.
- Blocked tasks are not escalated early.
- Delivery risks are often discovered late.
- Teams need better visibility into sprint readiness and progress.

Decisions:
- Build a prototype dashboard for agile delivery insights.
- Use AI to summarize sprint health and suggest next actions.
- Track blocked tasks, overdue tasks, completion rate, and assignee workload.
- Export reports for internal sharing.

Action Items:
- Talha will build the first Streamlit prototype.
- Sara will define reporting metrics.
- Ahsan will review dashboard layout.
- Fasi will test sample sprint data.
- Sahar will document limitations and future improvements.

Risks:
- API limits may affect AI response reliability.
- Sample data may not fully represent enterprise delivery workflows.
- Users may need training to trust AI-generated recommendations.

Future Ideas:
- Integrate with Jira API.
- Add Power BI export.
- Add Azure DevOps support.
- Generate release notes automatically.
- Create AI-generated sprint retrospectives.
